## 📖 About the Project

**Namma Thari** is a real small business in Surumanpatty, Kadavur, Karur — specializing in handcrafted natural fiber mats (bamboo, coconut, sampu, vetiver, valainaru). The business had no online presence before this website.

I built this site to:
- Give the family business its first digital storefront
- Showcase the product range with high-quality images
- Provide a single point of contact for inquiries from outside the region
- Demonstrate that traditional crafts can compete with modern eCommerce

**This is a live, in-use business website** — not a portfolio mockup. Real customers find the business through this site.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🏠 **Hero section** with branded video showcasing the craft process
- 🌱 **About section** telling the story of the family business and its commitment to sustainability
- 🛍️ **Product gallery** featuring 8+ natural fiber mat varieties with images
- 📞 **Contact section** with address, phone, email, and Instagram integration
- 📱 **Fully responsive** — works on every device, optimized for the mobile-first customer base in rural Tamil Nadu
- 🌐 **Deployed via GitHub Pages** — zero hosting cost for the family business
